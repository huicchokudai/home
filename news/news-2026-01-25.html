Islamic Cultural Seminar: Syria

Monday, January 25, 2026

The Hokkaido University Islamic Cultural Club, in collaboration with the Hokkaido Islamic Society, proudly presents the Islamic Cultural Seminar: Syria. This seminar features a special lecture by Prof. Dr. Ahmad Almansour (Keio University), exploring Syria’s rich cultural heritage and historical legacy. The program also includes Islamic cultural exhibitions and an opportunity for cross-cultural academic exchange.
Date: Sunday, January 25

Time: 11:00–13:00

Venue: Small Auditorium, Hokkaido University Academic Exchange Hall
We warmly welcome students, researchers, and members of the community to join this meaningful cultural and educational event.
