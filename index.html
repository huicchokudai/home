About HUICC

The Hokkaido University Islamic Cultural Club (HUICC) is a student organization dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of Islam, supporting Muslim students, and encouraging intercultural dialogue within the academic community of :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.

HUICC serves as a welcoming platform for students of diverse backgrounds to engage in educational, cultural, and social activities rooted in mutual respect and academic values.

Our Activities

  • Weekly study circles and discussions
  • Islamic lectures and seminars
  • Cultural exchange programs
  • Community outreach and social events
  • Support for Muslim students on campus

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote accurate understanding of Islam, encourage peaceful coexistence, and contribute positively to the multicultural environment of Hokkaido University through education, dialogue, and community service.
