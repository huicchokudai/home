About HUICC

The Hokkaido University Islamic Cultural Club (HUICC) is a student organization dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of Islamic Culture, supporting Muslim students, and encouraging intercultural dialogue within the academic community of Hokkaido University.

HUICC serves as a welcoming platform for students of diverse backgrounds to engage in educational, cultural, and social activities rooted in mutual respect and academic values.

Our Activities

  • Weekly study circles and discussions
  • Islamic lectures, workshops, and seminars
  • Cultural exchange programs
  • Community outreach and social events
  • Support for Muslim students on campus

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote an accurate understanding of Islam based on the Al-Qur'an and Sunnah, according to the understanding of the Prophet Muhammad's companions, encourage peaceful coexistence, and contribute positively to the multicultural environment of Hokkaido University through education, dialogue, and community service.
